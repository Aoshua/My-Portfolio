Writing Center

As a writing tutor at Weber State University, it was my responsibility, and pleasure, to help these students gain a better understanding of the writing process through one-on-one teaching. This is a job that I thoroughly enjoyed because I was able to help others learn new skills in research, organization, collaboration, and debate. Students entered tutoring sessions with questions and concerns about their writing projects. However, by the end of a session, the students’ questions are resolved, and they leave with a greater sense of what to do next in their projects. Along with my colleagues, I issued in a new chapter for the Writing Center through the implementation of digital rhetoric by teaching students to create editorials and documentaries via Adobe produces such as InDesign, PremierePro, and Spark. During my time at the Writing Center, I maintained a system using Google Forms and Google Sheets to track and congratulate my coworkers for their efforts and achievements.

(November 2017 to April 2019)
