About Me:

I am the kind of person who becomes passionate about whatever task lies before them. I attack problems in a methodical and timely manner, and I love the feeling of a job well done. Of the many interests that I have, technology rises to the top. I am a studying Software Engineer who hopes to help propel future innovation with AI and machine learning. I graduated with my Associates degree from Weber State University a week before I graduated from high school. I served an LDS mission in Argentina for two years. There, I picked up the Spanish language and learned the value of service. I am currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a minor in Math.
